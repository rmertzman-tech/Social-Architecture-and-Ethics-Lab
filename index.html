Definitions (Updated for Agential Physics)

Internalized Stress Load ($L_{GC}$)
This is the "Noise" experienced by the agent. It is NOT just the objective sound or chaos in the room; it is the subjective burden that has penetrated the agent's perception filters. Two agents in the same room may have different $L_{GC}$ based on their internal state.
Dynamic Sapolsky Limit (The Resilience Threshold)
The breaking point where an agent's prefrontal cortex shuts down and ethics go offline. In this model, the limit is Dynamic: as you increase the Metabolic Budget, you push this threshold higher, allowing the agent to remain coherent under much higher levels of noise.
Agential Entropy ($S_{agent}$)

The Strong Triangle Inequality (Friction)
A rule in P-Adic geometry stating that a group's distance is determined by its most outlier member. If one person in the room "truncates" (turns red), they create a massive friction spike that can crash the cooperation (Lambda) of the entire group.
